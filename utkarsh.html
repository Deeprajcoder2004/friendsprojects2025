SMIT Clubs and Societies

SMIT Cultural Club

  • Illusion - The oldest dance group on campus, inspiring students to express through dance since 2000.
  • Udaan - Founded in 2015, this vibrant dance society has taken the stage by storm with stunning performances.
  • Chromatics - SMIT’s first music club, bringing together diverse musical talents and creating magical performances since 2016.

SMITMUN

SMITMUN is a platform that simulates United Nations conferences, where students discuss global issues and develop leadership skills. This student-run initiative creates world-class leaders.

CHATTER

Chatter is SMIT’s student-run newsletter, covering everything from campus events to student life, food, and more. A creative outlet for the campus community to share ideas and stories.

SPORTS CLUB

The Sports Club encourages fitness and competitive spirit by organizing various sports events and promoting a healthy lifestyle within the campus.

PHOTOGRAPHY CLUB

The Photography Club at SMIT nurtures a passion for capturing moments and the art of photography, offering workshops and opportunities for creative expression.

INNOVISON

Innovison is a dynamic group of clubs that promotes innovation and creativity, featuring clubs like Edge Gaming, INQUIZITIVE, OSC, AI Club, and De Desino.

  • Edge Gaming - A gaming community that connects gamers and organizes competitive gaming tournaments.
  • INQUIZITIVE - A quiz club that fosters a love for knowledge through interactive quizzes and competitions.
  • Open Source Club (OSC) - Encourages students to contribute to open-source projects and build their development skills.
  • AI Club - Focuses on the study and exploration of Artificial Intelligence technologies and applications.
  • De Desino - A creative club for students interested in design, including graphic design, UX/UI, and more.

ALUMNI CELL

The Alumni Cell maintains strong connections between SMIT and its alumni, creating networking opportunities and ensuring that alumni stay engaged with the college.

DECODERS

Decoders is a club dedicated to coding enthusiasts. It fosters learning through coding challenges, collaborative projects, and skill-building workshops.
